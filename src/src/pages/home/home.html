A Tale of an Unnammed Hero

This is the unlikely story about a young man born in a small village outside of Wayrest in the hills of High Rock. At the age of 9, our hero lost his entire family in a horrific mass exodus caused by the legendary warrior only known as the Dragonborn.

10 years have passed and our hero is now working as a stable master's apprentice. Step into the life of our hero and experience life in Tamriel.
